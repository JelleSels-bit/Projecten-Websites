Fitness:

I started my fitness journey in the summer of 2023. My friend and I aim to work out three times a week (Monday, Wednesday, and Friday). Since we only go three times a week, we focus on a full-body workout. This means that over these three days, we target and train all the major muscle groups.

So far, the heaviest weight I've managed to push was on the supine bench press. it was 160 kg (about 352 lbs) for 12 reps. It's a big achievement for me, and I'm working hard to improve my strength and form each week.

Our workouts include exercises for the chest, back, shoulders, and core. We try to keep a balanced approach, making sure each muscle group gets enough attention without overtraining. In addition to the strength training, we also focus on proper warm-ups and cool-downs to prevent injuries and keep our bodies flexible.

My goal is to keep pushing my limits and hopefully reach new personal bests in the coming months. Staying consistent with the routine is challenging, but having a workout partner keeps me motivated and accountable.

My routine:

  • Chest exercises:
    - Inclined Dumbell press 3 x 12
    - Supine bench press 3 x 12
    - Pec Fly 3 x 12
  • Shoulder exercises:
    - Rotator cuff Inside 2 x 15 (lightweight)
    - Rotator cuff Outside 2 x 15 (lightweight)
  • Tricep exercises:
    - Tricep extension 3 x 12
    - Overhead Tricep extension 3 x12

  • Back exercises:
    - Lat pull down 3 x 12
    - Seated row 3 x 12
    - Dumbell row 3 x 12
  • Bicep exercises:
    - Hammer curls 3 x 12
    - One handed Preacher curl 3 x 12
    - Barbell curl 3 x 12

  • Back exercises:
    - Pull ups 3 x 12
    - Kroc row
  • Other exercises:
    - Supine bench press 3 x 12
    - Overhead cable curls 3 x 12
    - Diamond push ups To failure
    - Goblet Squat
